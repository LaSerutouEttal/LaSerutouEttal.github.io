about this page

I'm a programmer by trade, but my real interests lie in anime, art, video games, making music, and firearms as well as many other areas.

I have wanted to create a "personal website" for a long time. Part of this was an admiration for "web 1.0" styled "homepage culture". Part of this was a great excuse to hone my HTML/web development skillz. Part of this was a simple disillusionment with "web 2.0" ideas of social media and everyone having identical homepages on sites like facebook. Despite this, at the time of writing, I still participate in most of the common social media sites. But this page is to act as my one "true" homepage, that describes me better than any other page could.

-Alfscene 2016/3/23
